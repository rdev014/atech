Who We Are

AS Freight Logistics is a leading provider of comprehensive freight and logistics solutions, dedicated to facilitating the seamless movement of goods across the globe. With a strong commitment to reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses of all sizes, from small enterprises to multinational corporations.

Our Mission

At AS Freight Logistics, our mission is to deliver innovative and tailored logistics services that exceed our clients' expectations. We strive to optimize supply chain efficiency, reduce costs, and provide unparalleled customer support, ensuring that our clients can focus on their core business while we handle the complexities of transportation and logistics.

You are selecting a partner for life who will assist you with all of your transportation needs, not just a freight forwarding firm. As a multidisciplinary business, we offer truckload, LTL, intermodal, rail, ocean, and air freight services. We can ship anything, wherever you want to ship it.

Our Expertise

With years of industry experience and a team of highly skilled professionals, AS Freight Logistics offers expertise in all aspects of freight forwarding, customs brokerage, transportation management, warehousing, and distribution. Core Values

  • Customized Solutions:

    We understand that every business is unique. That's why we offer personalized solutions tailored to each client's specific needs, ensuring maximum efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

  • Technology-driven:
