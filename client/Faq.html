Key components of freight logistics include transportation, inventory management, warehousing, packaging, order processing, and distribution. Each component plays a crucial role in ensuring the smooth movement of goods through the supply chain.

Freight logistics utilizes various modes of transportation, including road transport (trucks), rail transport, air freight, ocean freight, and multimodal transport (combination of different modes). The choice of mode depends on factors such as distance, cost, speed, and nature of the goods being transported.
